Fertilizers are essential to our daily lives

With the global population projected to reach 10 billion by 2050, farmers must produce 55% more food in the next two decades. Given the limited availability of arable land, the solution lies in improving productivity on existing farmland—a challenge where the importance of fertilizer becomes indispensable. Efficient processing plays a crucial role in transforming agriculture and boosting food production by providing necessary raw materials.

The global fertilizer market size is anticipated to reach around USD 276.92 billion by 2034, expanding at a CAGR of 2.9%
