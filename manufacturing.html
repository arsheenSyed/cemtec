Precision and quality at every step

With a 1,100 m² assembly and production hall equipped with state-of-the-art machinery, we ensure the highest level of precision and efficiency—from individual component fabrication to final assembly

Setting the standard in industrial manufacturing

With decades of expertise and a commitment to continuous improvement, CEMTEC sets the standard for efficiency, precision, and reliability in industrial manufacturing.

Manufacturing in-house saves both costs and time, which is of particular benefit to our customers.

  • Advanced Equipment & Infrastructure – Our 75-ton overhead crane (optional 150 tons) enables safe and efficient handling of large components, supported by precision turning, drilling, and milling machines
  • End-to-End Solutions – From fabrication and pre-assembly to trial runs, quality control, packing, and shipping, we manage the entire process in-house
  • Uncompromising Quality Control – Rigorous inspections, intermediate storage of bought-in parts, and structured spare parts management ensure seamless operations
  • Flexibility & Service – In addition to manufacturing new components, we provide repair services, spare parts, and specialized tools and measuring devices for installation and commissioning
